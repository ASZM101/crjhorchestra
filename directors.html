Directors

Learn about the Directors of the Cinco Ranch Junior High Orchestra!

Laura Vu

Laura Vu is the Director of Orchestras at Cinco Ranch Junior High. She has been teaching at CRJH for a few years. Outside of school, Mrs. Vu is also a private teacher, and before she began teaching at Cinco Ranch Junior High, she was an orchestra director at other schools.

Contact Mrs. Vu

Barbara Jones

Barbara Jones is the Assistant Director of Orchestras at Cinco Ranch Junior High. She has been teaching at CRJH for a couple years. Outside of school, Ms. Jones is also a private lesson teacher for violinists and violists, and she has played with the Houston Symphony on numerous occasions.

Contact Ms. Jones
