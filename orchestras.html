Orchestras

Find out more about the different groups in the Cinco Ranch Junior High Orchestra!

First Year Orchestra

FYO Orchestra stands for “First Year Orchestra”. You learn the basics for starting to play an instrument and don’t need to have any experience to join this class. This class is made up of mostly 6th graders and some 7th and 8th graders.

Philharmonic Orchestra

Philharmonic Orchestra is the first level of the preforming orchestras. The Philharmonic Orchestra typically combines with the Chamber orchestra in class and during concerts. This class is made up of mostly 7th graders and some 6th and 8th graders.

Chamber Orchestra

Chamber Orchestra is the second level of the performing orchestras. The Chamber Orchestra typically combines with the Philharmonic Orchestra in class and during concerts. This class is made up of mostly 7th graders and some 6th and 8th graders.

Sinfonia Orchestra

Sinfonia Orchestra is the second highest level of the performing orchestras. This class is made up of mostly 7th and 8th graders and some 6th graders. Sinfonia students have a chance to audition for a place in the Cinco Symphony. (information in Symphony Orchestra description)

Symphony Orchestra

Symphony Orchestra is the highest level of the performing orchestras. This class is made up of mostly 8th graders and some 7th graders. Symphony students perform with band and choir in a group called Cinco Symphony.
