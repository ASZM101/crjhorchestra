Frequently Asked Questions

Question: Do I always have to rosin my bow?

Answer: No, you can rosin it every other day. You have to experiment with the amount of rosin you use to figure out how much you need.

Question: How much do I have to practice?

Answer: You can practice as much as you want and as much are you're able to practice! Challenge yourself, but don't push yourself too hard. You will always feel like you should practice more.

Good Practice Habits

Always wipe your strings when you're done practicing.


Create a schedule to help you use your practice time efficiently!


Try to maintain your best posture while playing.


Make goals for yourself. They're important for everyone!


Faster isn't always better. Take your time and work your way up. Slow practice is the best practice.


Remember to tighten your bow before playing!

Playing Tips

Have a nice, high left elbow for better intonation.


Notes are still out of tune? Play each note slowly with a tuner, and don't be afraid to add tapes!


Before playing, add rosin to your bow for a richer tone.


Is your sound weak? Move your bow closer to the bridge, and make sure to lean with your first finger for a stronger sound!
